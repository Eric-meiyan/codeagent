## Introduction

CodeAgent provides browser-based AI coding workspaces that run in cloud sandboxes. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect and how we use it to operate accounts, sessions, billing, support, and security.

## Information We Collect

1. **Account information** — Name, email address, login method, and profile details you provide.
2. **Workspace metadata** — Session names, runtime status, archive metadata, usage counters, and operational logs needed to restore or debug a session.
3. **Workspace content** — Files, terminal output, prompts, and generated code may be processed to provide the coding workspace and AI agent features.
4. **Billing data** — Subscription, credit, invoice, and payment-provider records. Full payment card data is handled by payment processors.
5. **Device and usage data** — Browser, device, IP-derived region, and product interaction events used for security, abuse prevention, and product reliability.

## How We Use Information

We use information to provide cloud workspaces, route model requests, restore sessions, calculate usage, process payments, improve reliability, answer support requests, and prevent abuse.

## Model Providers and Sandboxes

When you use hosted model access, prompts and context may be sent through CodeAgent's model gateway to configured upstream model providers. When you use your own model key, requests may be routed to the provider you configure. Workspace execution happens in isolated cloud runtimes and may be archived to private object storage for recovery.

## Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share data only with service providers that help operate CodeAgent, when required by law, or when needed to protect users, the product, or infrastructure.

## Security

We use access controls, transport encryption, private storage paths, audit logs, and key-management practices designed to keep model credentials out of user-readable runtime environments. No internet service can be guaranteed perfectly secure, but we work to reduce risk continuously.

## Your Choices

You may request access, correction, export, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us through the support channel listed on the website. Some operational records may be retained when required for security, billing, legal compliance, or abuse prevention.

## Changes

We may update this policy as CodeAgent evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above and, when appropriate, notifying users through the service.

## Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the contact address listed on the CodeAgent website.
